Illusrator Icon

This piece was created in Illustrator using the artist herself as inspiration for the design. The artist (Ingrid Smith) is known by her family for having a large quantity of hair and glasses so those are elements that were incorporated into the design. The color choices was made were made based on the artist's favorite color combination, red and black and the over all creaturesque look of the figure was chosen because of the artist's fondness of fantasy art.
