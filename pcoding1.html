Water Creature

Statement

Titled Water Creature this piece was coded using p5 Javascript. For the creature itself I coded a parent function then created several other functions within it to serve as its features, such as its body, eyes, and pupils. I used “translate” combined with “push and pop” to place each feature and I used “rotate” along with “mouseX, mouseY” in order to make it more interactive. After I finished I thought the overall piece looked a bit lonely so I added clouds and had them move across the screen using a counter to counteract the emptiness and make it a bit livelier.
