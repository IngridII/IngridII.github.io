Statement

For this piece I wanted to make a set of brushes and stamps that allowed the user to create their own minimalist landscape. Often people view painting landscapes as something difficult to do, so for this piece I broke down the landscape into very basic parts in the form of brushes. I believe that anyone who uses them should be able achieve something resembling a landscape.
