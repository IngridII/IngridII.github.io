Distopian Industrial Cityscape

Statement

This is a rendered image of my attempt at creating an environment using the software Blender. My goal for this piece was to create a dystopian industrial cityscape. To help me get the results I was after I used a series modifiers and sourced textures from online resources. I also wanted to convey a sense of unnaturalness and irregularity so I turned to lighting to give that overall effect to the piece.
